About CartPole

The CartPole problem is a classic reinforcement learning task. The goal is to balance a pole on a moving cart by applying left or right forces. The agent receives observations about the cart's position, velocity, pole angle, and angular velocity, and must learn to keep the pole upright for as long as possible.
